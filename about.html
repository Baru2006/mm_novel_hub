Sharing Myanmar's Stories

MyanWorld Studio was founded on a simple mission:

“We translate, create, and share Myanmar stories, folktales, and manga for all readers.”

We believe that stories are a bridge between cultures. Our passion is to bring the rich, diverse, and often untold narratives of Myanmar to a global audience. From ancient legends to modern tales, we are dedicated to quality storytelling and beautiful art.
